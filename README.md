# econ1626-ai-future-forecast
econ1626-ai-future-forecast
The Hollow Pyramid — AI and the Australian Legal Profession to 2030
An AI-2027-inspired interactive web essay forecasting how AI reshapes the
Australian legal services industry. Built for ECON1626, Assessment 3.
The thesis
> The automation of routine junior-level legal tasks is expected to cause a
> shortage of people qualified for senior roles by 2030 — unless legal demand
> grows or new training pathways are introduced. AI creates the shortage by
> automating the very tasks through which juniors acquire the skills, knowledge
> and judgment that senior roles require.

How it's built
A single self-contained `forecast.html` — no build step required.
Interactive scenario pyramid (vanilla JS + CSS) showing the firm structure inverting across years and scenarios.
Two interactive line charts (Chart.js via CDN) for junior intake and qualified senior supply under each scenario.
Typography: Fraunces (display), Inter (body), IBM Plex Mono (data), via Google Fonts.
AI-use note
Generative AI was used to assist with structuring, prose editing, and building
